# DailyFunding

## Description

DailyFunding is the organization or product umbrella currently associated in this wiki with the public `dailyfund-front` service and the internal `daily-publisher` publishing tool.

## Known Facts

- `dailyfund-front` is the user-facing web frontend for DailyFunding.
- `daily-publisher` is an internal publishing editor and deployment tool connected to DailyFunding content operations.
- Both related projects are modern Next.js 16 and React 19 applications in the current local review.
