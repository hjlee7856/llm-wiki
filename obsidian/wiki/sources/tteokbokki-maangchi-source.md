# Tteokbokki Source (Maangchi)

## Source

- https://www.maangchi.com/recipe/tteokbokki/comment-page-19

## Summary

This source documents a classic spicy tteokbokki recipe built around anchovy-kelp stock, garaetteok, gochujang, gochugaru, sugar, green onion, and optional fish cakes and boiled eggs.

## Key Claims

- Anchovy stock is treated as the main flavor base rather than plain water.
- The core sauce is a simple mixture of gochujang, gochugaru, and sugar.
- Fresh rice cakes cook faster, while frozen rice cakes should be thawed and soaked first.
- The dish is finished by simmering until the rice cakes soften and the sauce becomes glossy and thick.

## Evidence

- Source page title: `Hot and spicy rice cakes`.
- Recipe page updated on 2025-02-28.
- Ingredient list includes rice cakes, water, dried anchovies, dried kelp, gochujang, gochugaru, sugar, green onion, optional eggs, and optional fish cakes.
- Directions specify 15 minutes for stock and about 10 to 15 minutes for the final simmer.
