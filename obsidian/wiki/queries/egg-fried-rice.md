# Egg Fried Rice

## Question

How do you make simple egg fried rice?

## Answer

### Basic Version

Ingredients for 1 to 2 servings:

- 1 bowl cooked rice, preferably cold rice
- 2 eggs
- 2 to 3 tablespoons chopped green onion
- 1 to 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- salt and black pepper to taste

Steps:

1. Loosen the rice so it is not clumped.
2. Beat the eggs with a small pinch of salt.
3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat and add oil.
4. Stir-fry the green onion briefly until fragrant.
5. Add the egg and scramble it lightly.
6. Add the rice and stir-fry until the egg is evenly mixed in and the rice is heated through.
7. Add soy sauce around the edge of the pan and mix quickly.
8. Season with pepper and adjust salt if needed.
9. Serve immediately.

### Tips

- Cold rice works better than freshly steamed rice because it stays separate more easily.
- Use high heat briefly rather than low heat for a long time, to avoid soggy rice.
- Do not add too much soy sauce, or the rice becomes wet and heavy.
- Add ham, onion, carrot, peas, or garlic if a fuller version is wanted.
